What It Is

The Delta deserves to tell its own story.

Life in the Delta is a documentary-style community series created by Jase Herrin. It highlights local people, businesses, towns, traditions, events, history, humor, challenges, and everyday life across the Mississippi Delta.

The mission is simple: preserve memories, support local communities, and show viewers that some of the most meaningful stories are found close to home.
