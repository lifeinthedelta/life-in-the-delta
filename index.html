About the Series

Preserving the stories of the Delta

Living in the Delta is a documentary-style project focused on the communities, people, history, culture, landmarks, businesses, and everyday life of the Mississippi Delta.

The series begins in Cleveland, Boyle, and Ruleville, Mississippi, with plans to grow into more Delta communities over time.

Meet the Crew

The people behind the project

Living in the Delta is powered by volunteers who care about preserving the stories, landscapes, and communities of the Mississippi Delta.

JH

Jase Herrin

Creator • Executive Producer • Director • Cinematographer • Editor

Jase created Living in the Delta and oversees the project from planning and interviews to filming, editing, branding, and production. His goal is to preserve the people, places, history, and culture of the Mississippi Delta for future generations.

DB

Darrion Brent

Drone Operator I

Darrion serves as the project’s first volunteer drone operator, capturing cinematic aerial footage of Delta landscapes, communities, landmarks, farms, and waterways.
