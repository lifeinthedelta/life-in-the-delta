About the project

The Delta deserves to be remembered in its own voice.

Welcome to Living in the Delta

Living in the Delta is a documentary-style series focused on real communities, local history, small businesses, landmarks, farms, artists, families, and the everyday people who shape the Mississippi Delta.

The project begins in Cleveland, Boyle, and nearby communities, with plans to expand across the region as more stories are discovered.

The Mississippi Delta

Why this matters

Preserving stories before they disappear.

Every town has a history. Every family has a story. Every road, field, church, storefront, and front porch holds memories worth documenting.
