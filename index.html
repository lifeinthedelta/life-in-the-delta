About the Show

Welcome to the Delta.

Life in the Delta is a reality-style documentary series created by Jase Herrin. The show follows everyday life while spotlighting the people, local businesses, events, traditions, and hidden gems that give the Mississippi Delta its heart.

This is not scripted drama. It is a celebration of genuine personalities, hometown pride, hard work, humor, friendship, and community.
