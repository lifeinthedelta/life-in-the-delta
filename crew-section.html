Meet the Crew

Living in the Delta

Behind every story is a dedicated team of volunteers passionate about documenting life across the Mississippi Delta. Meet the people helping bring Living in the Delta to life.

JH

Jase Herrin

Creator • Executive Producer • Director • Cinematographer • Editor

Founder of Living in the Delta and the driving force behind the series. Jase oversees every part of production—from planning episodes and conducting interviews to filming, editing, and managing the project. His goal is to preserve the stories, people, history, and culture of the Mississippi Delta for future generations.

DB

Darrion Brent

Drone Operator I

Darrion serves as the project’s first volunteer drone operator, capturing cinematic aerial footage of the Delta’s landscapes, communities, landmarks, farms, and waterways. His footage helps showcase the beauty and scale of the region from a unique perspective.
